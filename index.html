About Us

Christian Velez

Based in Shamokin, PA, Prime Reflections Detailing is dedicated to providing exceptional automotive care services. With professional training from UTI Exton, I bring technical expertise and a passion for perfection to every vehicle. Best of all, we come to you!

Whether it's a daily driver, a classic car, or a motorcycle, each vehicle receives personalized attention and premium products to achieve outstanding results. From full details to interior cleaning and premium exterior washes, we offer flexible pricing based on your vehicle size. Your satisfaction and your vehicle's appearance are my top priorities.
